About Me

I am a freelance video and photo editor who began my creative journey in 2024, specializing in producing engaging and polished content using Canva and CapCut pro. With a strong passion for visual storytelling, I focus on transforming raw photos and videos into high-quality materials tailored for social media, marketing, and personal branding. My goal is to deliver creative, professional, and impactful content that helps clients effectively share their stories.
